MAJOR CHURN DRIVERS IDENTIFIED:
================================================================================

1. CUSTOMER SERVICE INTERACTIONS (Strongest Positive Driver)
   Effect Size (Cohen's d): +0.596 (Medium-to-Large Effect)
   Statistical Significance: p < 0.001 (Highly Significant)
   
   Findings:
   • Churned customers: 3.18 interactions on average
   • Retained customers: 2.49 interactions on average
   • Difference: +69% more interactions for churned customers
   
   IMPLICATION: Customers who contact support more frequently are significantly
   more likely to churn. This suggests unresolved issues or persistent problems
   with the service.
   
   RECOMMENDATION: Improve first-contact resolution rates, proactively address
   recurring issues, and implement customer satisfaction surveys after support
   interactions.


2. TOTAL VIEWING HOURS (Strongest Negative Driver)
   Effect Size (Cohen's d): -0.521 (Medium Effect)
   Statistical Significance: p < 0.001 (Highly Significant)
   Feature Importance Rank: #1 (23.8% importance)
   
   Findings:
   • Churned customers: 66.6 hours/month average
   • Retained customers: 83.2 hours/month average  
   • Difference: -16.6 hours (20% less engagement)
   
   IMPLICATION: Low engagement is a critical churn predictor. Customers watching
   less content are deriving less value from the service.
   
   RECOMMENDATION: Implement engagement campaigns targeting users who fall below
   70 hours/month. Consider personalized content recommendations and email
   campaigns to re-engage low-activity users.


3. BINGE WATCHING SESSIONS (Strong Engagement Indicator)
   Effect Size (Cohen's d): -0.500 (Medium Effect)
   Statistical Significance: p < 0.001 (Highly Significant)
   Feature Importance Rank: #5 (11.2% importance)
   
   Findings:
   • Churned customers: 6.2 sessions/month average
   • Retained customers: 7.7 sessions/month average
   • Difference: -1.5 sessions (20% fewer)
   
   IMPLICATION: Binge-watching behavior indicates high content satisfaction and
   platform stickiness. Lower binge-watching correlates with higher churn.
   
   RECOMMENDATION: Promote binge-worthy content (complete series, related content
   queues). Use auto-play features and "continue watching" prompts strategically.
